What is Ziwei Doushu (Purple Star Astrology)?

Ziwei Doushu, also known as Purple Star Astrology, is an important branch of traditional Chinese fortune-telling that originated during the Tang and Song dynasties. It uses the Purple Star as the main reference, combined with twelve palace positions and numerous stars, to form a complete system of life analysis. By analyzing the star arrangement corresponding to one's birth time, it interprets various aspects of life, including personality, career, relationships, and health.

Is my birth information secure?

Absolutely secure. Your birth information is only used for generating charts and AI readings. We use bank-level encryption technology to protect your data. We will not sell or share your personal information with any third party. For details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

How accurate are the AI readings?

Ziwei Spark's AI readings are based on traditional Ziwei Doushu theory combined with modern artificial intelligence technology. The readings are for reference and entertainment purposes only and should not be used as the sole basis for major life decisions. Your destiny is in your own hands; our readings are meant to provide direction and reference for your thoughts.

How do I change my birth information?

You can modify your birth information by tapping "Birth Information" in the "Profile" page. After modification, the system will regenerate your chart. Please ensure the birth time entered is accurate, as this directly affects the chart generation results.

Which devices are supported?

Ziwei Spark currently supports iPhone and iPad running iOS 16.0 or later. We continuously optimize the app experience to ensure smooth operation on all devices.
