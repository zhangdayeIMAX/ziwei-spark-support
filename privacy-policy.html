To provide Zi Wei Dou Shu (Purple Star Astrology) analysis services, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: User identifier obtained through Sign in with Apple

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifier, etc.
  • Purchase Information: In-app purchase transaction records

3. Use of Information

The information we collect will be used for the following purposes:

  • Generate and analyze your Zi Wei Dou Shu chart
  • Provide personalized AI-powered astrological readings
  • Store and sync your chat history for cross-device access
  • Manage your account and provide customer support
  • Process in-app purchase transactions
  • Improve our products and service quality
  • Send you service-related notifications
Special Note About Chat History:

Your chat history is stored on our servers to provide history functionality and cross-device synchronization. We may analyze anonymized conversation data to improve AI service quality, but we will not use your personal conversations for third-party advertising or sale. You can delete your chat history at any time within the app.

4. Information Storage and Protection

We take the following measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Sensitive information is encrypted at rest on our servers
  • Strict access control mechanisms
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

Your data is stored on secure cloud servers. We retain your information in accordance with legal requirements and business needs.

5. Information Sharing

We commit not to sell your personal information. We may only share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent

6. Third-Party Services

Our application may use the following third-party services:

  • Sign in with Apple: For account authentication

These third-party services have their own privacy policies, which we recommend you review.

7. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal bases for processing your personal data include:

  • Contract Performance: Processing your birth information and account information is necessary to provide our astrological analysis services
  • Consent: You explicitly consent to our storage and processing of your chat history to provide cross-device synchronization
  • Legitimate Interests: Improving our services, ensuring security, and preventing fraud
  • Legal Obligations: Compliance with applicable laws and regulations

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: View the personal information we have collected about you
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): Request deletion of your personal information and account
  • Right to Restriction: Request restriction of processing of your personal data
  • Right to Data Portability: Obtain a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent to information processing at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing prior to withdrawal
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with the data protection supervisory authority in your country

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data according to the following principles:

  • Account Information: Retained during the active period of your account, deleted within 30 days after account deletion
  • Chat History: Retained until you actively delete it or close your account
  • Chart Data: Consistent with your account lifecycle
  • Transaction Records: Retained for the period required by law (typically 7 years)
  • Log Data: Retained for up to 90 days for security and troubleshooting purposes

Your personal data may be transferred to and stored on servers outside your country/region. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), your data may be transferred to countries outside the EEA. In such cases, we implement appropriate safeguards, including:

  • Using Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission
  • Ensuring the recipient country has an adequate level of data protection
  • Implementing supplementary technical and organizational measures to protect your data

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Union). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
